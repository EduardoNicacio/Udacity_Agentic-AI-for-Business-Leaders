# Rubric

Use this project rubric to understand and assess the project criteria.

## Step 2: Find and Fix a bug

### Criteria

Trace the functional flow of an Agentic AI system

### Submission Requirements

The location of the part of the system that is causing problems is identified, and the problem is described accurately.

### Criteria

Propose a technical fix for an Agentic AI problem

### Submission Requirements

A solution to resolve the issue is described in 1-3 sentences.

## Step 3: Add Human Review

### Criteria

Determine how to introduce human review into an Agentic AI system

### Submission Requirements

- The _**Location**_ section describes the part of the system where the human should be consulted.
- The _**Decision**_ section lists the decisions the human could make.

### Criteria

Define the data necessary to perform human review

### Submission Requirements

The _**Data**_ section lists the data the human will need to make a high-quality decision and explains where the system will get it.

## Step 4: Ensure Customer Privacy

### Criteria

Analyze an Agentic AI system for privacy protection

### Submission Requirements

The _**Location**_ section identifies **five** places where data privacy could be compromised in the current system architecture.

### Criteria

Define how to secure customer data to maintain privacy standards

### Submission Requirements

The _**Solution**_ section describes the changes that need to be made to ensure that the system meets the required privacy standards. This may include multiple components, connections, or other privacy and security factors.

## Step 5: Extend the Workflow

### Criteria

Define how to add new capabilities to an existing Agentic AI system

### Submission Requirements

The _**Capability**_ section describes the functionality of the customer-requested enhancement that will be implemented (from the provided list)

The _**Location**_ section explains which part of the system is involved and which data it will use.

### Criteria

Determine the business value of a new Agentic AI capability

### Submission Requirements

The Business Value section lists at least one tangible benefit the new capability will provide to the company. These benefits can be intended for internal or external stakeholders or customers.

## Step 6: Estimate Operational Cost Drivers

### Criteria

Analyze an Agentic AI system to determine potentially expensive cost drivers

### Submission Requirements

At least two operational cost drivers are represented in the summary table.

## Suggestions to Make Your Project Stand Out

**Design a new agentic system from scratch to solve at least 3 challenges in the following scenario**.

### Scenario

You are a pastry chef who has built the most popular food truck brand in the city. The local market is saturated so you want to expand nationally.

### Challenges

- Create an agentic system to do the following:
  - Research and rank the top 20 food truck markets nationwide based on several success factors
  - For each market:
    - Research the best neighborhoods and local events for food trucks and create a map
    - Research local health and business licensing requirements and create a todo list
    - Research local food writers and bloggers, and draft intro emails that highlight news and media of your achievements so far
    - Research local cooking schools and draft intro emails to entice their best students to interview with you
- Track the price of labor, gas, kitchen rental, and food ingredients across all markets, and alert you when average prices increase too quickly
- Create a conversational voice service to help customers plan parties based on a pastry theme, and book picnic spaces at nearby parks.
- Create a paid pastry chef advice service to help home cooks brainstorm recipe ideas, assemble the necessary ingredients and equipment, and coach them through the baking process. Include automated purchasing and delivery of the ingredients via Instacart or a similar service.
  
Or if you prefer, feel free to define and solve your own challenge!
