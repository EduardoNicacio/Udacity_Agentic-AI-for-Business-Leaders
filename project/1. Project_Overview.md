# Project Overview

## Scenario

Congratulations! You have just been hired as the product leader who is responsible for a legacy industrial manufacturer's AI transformation.

Your first priority is to modernize the internal productivity tools, starting with the expense reporting system. Everyone hates the current process, which requires employees to fill out cumbersome forms, attach all the paper receipts, and wait weeks for reimbursement.

You are excited about the potential of Agentic AI to streamline the process dramatically, but also aware of the potential risks.

## Activities

Guide the team through the process of developing and extending its first Agentic AI system. Specifically, you will need to:

- Find and fix a bug
- Add human review
- Ensure customer privacy
- Extend the workflow
- Estimate operational cost

Your team is waiting. Let's get started.
